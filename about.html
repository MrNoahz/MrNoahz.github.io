About Me

Some Answers About Me

I'm a student, developer, designer, all of these things. But most importantly, I'm not a space lizard.

Hi, I'm Noah. I began to learn programming 3 years ago. I began as a popular mod creator in the Minecraft Pocket Edition community. Soon afterwards, I progressed to Android and Java applications. Arduino and microcontrollers came next, a fun adventure.

And now, I am exploring full stack web development, hoping to one day become a professional developer.

Apart from the time I spend learning, I enjoy reading (1984 is my favorite, tweet me yours), solving my Rubik's cube, swimming, and traveling.
I've been all over the world from Greece to Russia to Spain to Japan to Cambodia to Vietnam to Norway and much much more.
I have experience leading as Senior Patrol Leader for my Boyscout troop on campouts in Switzerland, klondikes in Norway, and hikes in Germany.

As well as my forthcoming aims, I hope to go down the path of either machine learning, computer vision, or cybersecurity.
